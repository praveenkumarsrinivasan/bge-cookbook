# Pork Ribs

## Overview

Hot-and-fast ribs with a mustard binder, a short smoke, and a buttery wrap before glazing.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked pork ribs
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: about 3 hours
- Total time: about 3 hours 15 minutes
- Serves: 4-6
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 350 F (177 C)

## Ingredients

Ribs:

- 2 racks pork ribs

Seasoning:

- Mustard
- BAMM
- Cimarron Docs Sweet Ribs

Spritz:

- Apple cider vinegar

Butter bath:

- Butter cubes
- Extra seasoning
- BBQ sauce (optional)

## Method

1. Remove the membrane, then coat ribs with mustard and seasoning.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 350 F (177 C).
3. Smoke for about 1 hour 30 minutes, spritzing with apple cider vinegar every 30-45 minutes, until about 160 F (71 C) internal.
4. Wrap ribs in foil with butter cubes and extra seasoning (and sauce if desired). Cook about 1 hour 15 minutes to around 200 F (93 C) internal.
5. Glaze with sauce for 10-15 minutes, targeting about 206 F (97 C) internal.
6. Rest briefly before slicing.

## Notes

- Inspired by Malcom Reed and Heath Riles.
- Smoking Dad BBQ and Bar-A-BBQ also influenced the cold smoke technique.
