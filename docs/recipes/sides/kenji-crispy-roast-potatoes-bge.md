# Kenji-Style Crispy Roast Potatoes

## Overview

Classic crispy roast potatoes adapted for the Big Green Egg's dry, even heat. Parboiling with baking soda creates a starchy paste that crisps aggressively in the cooker.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Side
- Cuisine: American
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: 70 minutes
- Total time: 85 minutes
- Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients
- 2 quarts (2 l) water
- 2 kg (4 lb) potatoes(best: Russet or Yukon Gold), peeled and cut into large chunks
- 2 tbsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 6 tbsp (90 ml) extra-virgin olive oil, duck fat, beef drippings, or bacon fat
- 6 medium cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp fresh rosemary leaves, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley leaves, finely chopped
