# Pulled Lamb Leg

## Overview

Slow-smoked lamb leg finished in a stock-and-aromatics bath until pull-apart tender.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked and braised lamb leg
- Cuisine: BBQ
- Prep time: 15 minutes
- Cook time: about 6 hours
- Total time: about 7 hours
- Serves: 6-8
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 275 F (135 C)

## Ingredients

Lamb:

- 1 lamb leg

Seasoning:

- Mustard
- BAMM
- SPG

Butter bath:

- 500 ml beef, lamb, or chicken stock
- Lemon rind
- Garlic
- Onion

## Method

1. Coat the lamb leg with mustard, then season with BAMM and SPG.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 275 F (135 C).
3. Smoke until the internal temperature reaches about 167 F (75 C), roughly 3 hours.
4. Move the lamb into a foil tray with the stock, lemon rind, garlic, and onion. Cover with foil.
5. Continue cooking on the Egg (or in the oven) until about 204 F (96 C) internal, roughly 3 hours more.
6. Rest for about 1 hour, then pull and serve.
