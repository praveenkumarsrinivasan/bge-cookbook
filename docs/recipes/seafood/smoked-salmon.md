# Smoked Salmon

## Overview

Quick, gently smoked salmon with lemon, garlic, and pepper seasoning.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked salmon
- Cuisine: Seafood
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: about 20 minutes
- Total time: about 25 minutes
- Serves: 2-4
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 250 F (121 C)

## Ingredients

Salmon:

- 1 salmon fillet
- Oil
- Fresh lemon rind
- Suckle Busters Lemon Pepper Garlic (or similar seasoning)

## Method

1. Coat the salmon with oil, then season with lemon rind and lemon pepper garlic seasoning.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 250 F (121 C).
3. Smoke until the internal temperature reaches about 135 F (57 C), roughly 20 minutes.

## Notes

- USDA safe minimum for fish is 145 F (63 C) at the thickest point.
- Chef-preferred medium is 125-130 F (52-54 C) for moist, flaky results.
- Lean salmon can be cooked to about 120 F (49 C).
