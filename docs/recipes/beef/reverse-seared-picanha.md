# Reverse Seared Picanha

## Recipe Essentials

Serves: 8

- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 40 minutes
- Total time: 50 minutes
- EGG setup: two-zone grilling (indirect then direct)
- EGG target temperature: 250 F (121 C) indirect, 500 F (260 C) for sear

## Overview

Reverse-seared picanha for a Brazilian steakhouse-style roast on the Big Green Egg.

## Ingredients

- 2.5 lb (1.1 kg) picanha roast
- BBQ rub (or salt, pepper, and garlic)

## Method

1. Preheat the Big Green Egg to 250 F (121 C) using a two-zone setup.
2. Trim the silver skin and extra fat from the meat side of the picanha.
3. Crosshatch the fat cap.
4. Season both sides with your rub.
5. Grill the roast on the indirect side until the internal temperature reaches 120 F (49 C).
6. Open the vents to bring the direct side to about 500 F (260 C).
7. Sear fat side down for 90 seconds, then fat side up for 90 seconds.
8. Rest for 10 minutes, slice, and serve.
