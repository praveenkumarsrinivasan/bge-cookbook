# Chicken Drumsticks

## Overview

Indirect-smoked drumsticks finished with a quick buffalo-style butter and hot sauce glaze.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked chicken drumsticks
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 50-70 minutes
- Total time: about 1 hour
- Serves: 4
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 275-300 F (135-149 C)

## Ingredients

Chicken:

- 2-3 lb (0.9-1.4 kg) chicken drumsticks

Seasoning:

- BAMM
- Chicken Rub
- Oil or mustard

Sauce:

- 4 Tbsp butter
- 4 Tbsp Frank's RedHot sauce

## Method

1. Coat drumsticks with oil or mustard, then season with BAMM and Chicken Rub.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 275-300 F (135-149 C).
3. Cook until the internal temperature reaches 170 F (77 C), flipping after 20-30 minutes.
4. Brush with the butter and hot sauce glaze and cook 10 minutes more, or until 175 F (79 C).
