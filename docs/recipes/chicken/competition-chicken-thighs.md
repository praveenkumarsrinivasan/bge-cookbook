# Competition Chicken Thighs

## Overview

Indirect-smoked thighs finished in a butter bath, then glazed for a sticky competition-style finish.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked chicken thighs
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: about 1 hour 45 minutes
- Total time: about 2 hours
- Serves: 6-8
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 300 F (149 C)

## Ingredients

Chicken:

- 3-4 lb (1.4-1.8 kg) chicken thighs

Seasoning:

- BAMM
- Chicken Rub
- Tail Gators

Butter bath:

- Butter
- Extra seasoning

## Method

1. Coat thighs with oil, then season with BAMM, Chicken Rub, and Tail Gators.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 300 F (149 C).
3. Smoke for about 45 minutes.
4. Move thighs into a butter bath with extra seasoning and cook about 50 minutes more.
5. Glaze for 10 minutes, targeting an internal temperature near 205 F (96 C).
