# Chicken Wings

## Overview

Indirectly cooked wings finished with a simple butter and Frank's RedHot glaze.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked chicken wings
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 50-70 minutes
- Total time: about 1 hour
- Serves: 4
- EGG setup: indirect or double indirect
- EGG target temperature: 350 F (177 C)

## Ingredients

Wings:

- 2-3 lb (0.9-1.4 kg) chicken wings

Seasoning:

- BAMM
- Chicken Rub
- Oil or mustard

Wing sauce:

- 4 Tbsp butter
- 4 Tbsp Frank's RedHot sauce

## Method

1. Coat wings with oil or mustard, then season with BAMM and Chicken Rub.
2. Set the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 350 F (177 C).
3. Cook wings 20-30 minutes per side.
4. Glaze with wing sauce and cook 10-15 minutes more.
5. Target an internal temperature of about 175 F (79 C).
