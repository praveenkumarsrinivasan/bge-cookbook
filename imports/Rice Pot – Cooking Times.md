# Le Creuset Rice Pot – Cooking Times & Ratios

This guide provides **reliable stovetop cook times and water ratios** for cooking rice in a **Le Creuset Rice Pot** using the **absorption method**.

Cast iron retains heat extremely well, so gentle simmering and resting are key for perfect results.

---

## Standard Cooking Method (Absorption)

1. Rinse rice until water runs mostly clear  
2. Add rice, water, and a pinch of salt to the rice pot  
3. Bring to a **gentle boil** over medium heat  
4. Reduce heat to **very low**, cover with both lids  
5. Simmer for the required time (see table below)  
6. Turn off heat and **rest covered**  
7. Fluff with a fork or rice paddle before serving  

---

## Rice Cooking Times & Water Ratios

| Rice Type | Water : Rice Ratio | Simmer Time | Rest Time | Texture Notes |
|---------|-------------------|------------|-----------|---------------|
| White Rice (long grain) | 1.5-2.1 : 1 | 15–20 min | 10 min | Light, fluffy |
| Basmati Rice | 1.5-2.1 : 1 | 15–20 min | 10 min | Separate grains |
| Jasmine Rice | 1.5-2.1 : 1 | 15–18 min | 10 min | Soft, aromatic |
| Short-Grain / Sushi Rice | 1.25–1.4 : 1 | 15–18 min | 10 min | Sticky, glossy |
| Brown Rice | 2 : 1 | 35–45 min | 10 min | Nutty, chewy |
| Wild Rice | 3–4 : 1 | 40–45 min | 10 min | Firm, split grains |
| Mixed Rice (white + brown) | 2 : 1 | 40 min | 10 min | Balanced texture |

---

## Heat Control Tips (Important)

!!! tip
    Once boiling, **reduce heat aggressively**.  
    Le Creuset cast iron holds heat longer than stainless steel or aluminium.

!!! warning
    Do not lift the lid while simmering — trapped steam is essential for even cooking.

---

## Texture Adjustments

| Preference | Adjustment |
|---------|-----------|
| Softer rice | Add more water |
| Firmer rice | Reduce water slightly |
| Rice sticks to base | Lower heat earlier |
| Rice undercooked | Sprinkle 2–3 tbsp water, cover, steam 5 more minutes |

---

## Capacity Guidance (2L Rice Pot)

| Uncooked Rice | Servings |
|-------------|----------|
| 1 cup | 2–3 |
| 2 cups | 4–6 |
| 3 cups (max recommended) | 6–8 |

---

## Cleaning & Care

- Allow pot to cool before washing  
- Avoid metal utensils on enamel  
- Soak briefly if starch residue sticks  

---

## Quick Reference

- **White rice:** 15–18 min simmer  
- **Brown rice:** 35–45 min simmer  
- **Always rest:** 10 min covered  
- **Heat:** lower than you think  

---

*Works perfectly on gas, induction, electric, and ceramic cooktops.*
