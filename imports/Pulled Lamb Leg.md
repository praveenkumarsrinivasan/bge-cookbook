# Pulled Lamb Leg

## Seasoning
- Mustard
- BAMM
- SPG

## Butter bath
- 500 ml beef, lamb, or chicken stock
- Lemon rind
- Garlic
- Onion

## Method
- Set up the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 275°F (135°C).
- Smoke until internal temp of 167°F (75°C) approx 3 hours
- Place in a foil tray, add the butter bath and lamb, and cover with foil.
- Use the Big Green Egg or oven to cook until internal temp of 204°F (96°C), approx 3 hours.
- Target temp: 204°F (96°C), approx 6 hours.
- Rest for 1 hour
