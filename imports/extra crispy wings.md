# Extra Crispy Wings 

## Ingredients
- 2 lb (900 g) chicken wings, split into drumettes and flats
- 2 tsp (10 g) baking powder (aluminum‑free)
- 2 tsp (10 g) kosher salt (use half as much by volume if table salt)
- 2 tsp (10 g) cornstarch
- 4 Tbsp (50 g) unsalted butter
- 2 Tbsp (30 ml) Frank’s RedHot per pound of wings
- Blue cheese dressing + celery sticks

## Method
- Pat wings very dry. Toss with baking powder and salt until evenly coated; arrange on a wire rack over a foil‑lined sheet.
- Refrigerate uncovered 8–24 hours to dry‑brine.
- Roast at 450°F (230°C), flip once halfway, until deeply browned and crisp, about 40–50 minutes total.
- Warm butter + hot sauce, toss wings, serve. ([seriouseats.com](https://www.seriouseats.com/the-best-buffalo-wings-oven-fried-wings-recipe))

## Notes:
- Add 1 tsp cornstarch per pound of wings along with the salt + baking powder.
