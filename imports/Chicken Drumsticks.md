# Chicken Drumsticks

## Seasoning
- BAMM
- Chicken rub
- Oil or mustard

## Sauce
- 4 Tbsp butter
- Frank's RedHot sauce

## Method
- Set up the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 275-300°F (135-149°C).
- Cook until the internal temperature reaches 170°F (77°C).
- Flip after 20-30 minutes.
- Glaze and cook for 10 minutes more, or until 175°F (79°C).
