# Pork Ribs

## Seasoning
- BAMM
- Cimarron Docs Sweet Ribs
- Mustard

## Method
- Remove membrane, apply mustard and seasoning.
- Set up the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 350°F (177°C). Similar process for a Weber kettle.
- Smoke for 1 hour 30 minutes, or until 160°F (71°C) internal.
- Spray apple cider vinegar every 30-45 minutes.
- Wrap in foil for a butter bath, face down, for 1 hour 15 minutes or until 200°F (93°C) internal.
  - Butter cubes
  - Seasoning
  - Apple cider spray if needed
  - Sauce if needed
- Glaze in sauce for 10-15 minutes.
- Target temp: 206°F (97°C).
- Rest the meat.

## Notes
- Smoke for 1 hour 30 minutes at 350°F (177°C) until 160°F (71°C) internal.
- Butter bath for 1 hour 15 minutes at 350°F (177°C) until 200°F (93°C) internal.
- Glaze for 15 minutes.
- Total time: about 3 hours.
