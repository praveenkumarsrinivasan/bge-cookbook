# Smoked Salmon

## Seasoning
- Fresh lemon rind
- SuckleBusters Lemon Pepper Garlic (use desired seasoning)
- Oil

## Method

- Set up the Big Green Egg for indirect or double indirect cooking at 250°F (121°C).
- Smoke until internal temp of 135°F (57°C).
- Cook time: about 20 minutes.

## Notes
- USDA safe minimum: 145°F (63°C) for safety in the thickest part of the fish.
- Medium (chef preferred): 125-130°F (52-54°C) for moist, tender, flaky results.
- Lean salmon can be cooked to 120°F (49°C).
- Visual Check: The flesh should turn opaque and flake easily with a fork when done.
